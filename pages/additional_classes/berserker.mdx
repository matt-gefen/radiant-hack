# Berserker

A fighter who can harness their battle rage.

**Starting HP :** d12 + 4\
**HP Per Level/Resting (HD):** 1d12\
**Weapons & Armor :** Any and Gambeson, Leather\
**Attack Damage :** 1d10 / 1d8 Unarmed or Improvising

## Equipment
Every Berserker starts with one of the following sets of equipment:\
A. Leather  (2 Armour), two hand-axes, 2d10 coins, worn map of the area, horn of mead (Ud6)\
B. Hide Shirt and wolf fur cloak (1 Armour), greatsword (two-handed weapon), 4d6 coins,  

## Special features

Once per hour, whilst in combat, a Berserker can regain d10 lost **HP**.

As part of their action a Berserker can make 1 attack per level. One of those attacks can be done with advantage.

## Leveling up

Roll to see if attributes increase, roll twice for **STR** *and* **DEX**.

Gain 1HD - Roll 1d12 with Advantage to gain that many additional maximum HP.
